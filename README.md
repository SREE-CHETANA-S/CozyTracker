# ☕ CozyTracker 

A beautiful, responsive, and privacy-first student productivity dashboard designed to help you manage your academic and personal life all in one cozy place.

## ✨ Features

- **🎓 Smart Attendance Tracking:** Input your college timetable and track your attendance per subject. Set a custom attendance goal (e.g., 75% or 80%) and instantly see how many classes you can afford to skip or need to attend to stay on track.
- **🚀 Skill Development:** Log the skills you are learning outside of your curriculum, set goals, and visually track your progress.
- **💪 Health & Habit Tracker:** Build daily habits, track streaks, and log custom health metrics (like sleep, water intake, or steps) with fun emojis and customizable colors.
- **📅 Important Dates:** Keep an eye on upcoming exams, assignment deadlines, and college events.
- **📋 To-Do List:** A categorized task manager to separate your study tasks from personal errands.
- **🌓 Light & Dark Mode:** Switch between a sleek, modern dark mode and a warm, elegant light mode (featuring the *Playfair Display* font) to match your mood.
- **📱 Fully Responsive:** Carefully designed to look and feel like a native app on mobile devices.
- **🔒 Privacy First:** Your data is 100% yours. Everything is saved locally in your browser using `localStorage`, meaning no accounts are required and your data never leaves your device.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **HTML5** for semantic structure
- **CSS3** for styling, CSS variables, and responsive design (Flexbox/Grid)
- **Vanilla JavaScript** for DOM manipulation, state management, and local storage persistence
- **Tabler Icons** for beautiful, lightweight iconography
- **Google Fonts** (Sora & Playfair Display)

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Simply open the `index.html` file in any modern web browser.
2. Click **"Set up profile"** in the top right to enter your name, year, and personal attendance goal.
3. Start adding your subjects, timetable slots, and daily habits!

*(If you are viewing this on GitHub Pages, simply click the link in the "About" section on the right to view the live dashboard!)*
